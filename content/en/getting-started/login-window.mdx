# Login Window

<FigureProvider>
Log in to the system via the **Log in** window by entering your **username**, **password** and the **OTP**.

STEPS

Step 01: Navigate to the iFSAH platform.

<Figure
  baseName="login-window/login-page"
  alt="login-page"
  description="Login Page"
/>

Step 02: Enter your **username**.

<Figure
  baseName="login-window/user-credentials"
  alt="user-credentials"
  description="User Credentials Page"
/>

Step 03: Enter your **password**. If you wish to view it, click the eye icon.

Step 04: Click the **Sign In** button. You will be redirected to the OTP verification page.

Step 05: Enter the **OTP** received via email to log into the system.

<Figure
  baseName="login-window/otp-verification"
  alt="otp-verification"
  description="OTP Verification Page"
/>

Step 06: After successful login, you will be directed to the Dashboard.

<Figure
  baseName="login-window/dashboard"
  alt="dashboard"
  description="Dashboard Page"
/>
