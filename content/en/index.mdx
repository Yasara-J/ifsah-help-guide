# Introduction

**iFSAH** unifies disclosure operations into a **single, secure, and configurable platform,** enabling faster compliance, reduced operational friction, and long-term scalability.

## Features [#features]

- **Single Unified Platform** for all disclosure operations.

- **Fully Configurable Workflows & Forms** with minimal IT dependency.

- **Advanced Action & Notification Management.**

- **Centralized Admin & Issuer Management.**

- **Real-Time Executive Dashboard & Insights.**

- **Enhanced Security** with 2FA and reCAPTCHA.

- **Scalable Architecture** for future growth.
